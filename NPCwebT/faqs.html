FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions.

Why can appointments only be booked on Monday's and Thursday's?
Due to a current shortage in staff, it has been made necessary that we limit the amount of days we see to pets.
The original schedule will return once we have new medical workers in place. If you're interested
in applying for a position, please contact us.

What is the maximum age a dog can be for your clinic?
Unfortunately, we do not work with older pets. The acceptable age-range for a dog
is 1 to 12 years of age.

Will I be charged more if my pet is older?
We charge clients based on the age of their pet. The more the pet's age nears the max acceptable age,
the more the client will be charged. For more information please get in touch.
